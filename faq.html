Frequently Asked Questions

General & Orders

What materials are used in your Lippan Art?

Auraloom pieces are crafted using authentic Kutch clay sourced from Gujarat. We use high-quality, water-resistant MDF boards as a base for durability, along with real glass mirrors (Aabhla) to create the signature reflective effect.

How do I clean and maintain the artwork?

Since our art is made of clay relief work, do not use water or wet cloth. We recommend gently dusting it with a soft-bristle paintbrush or a dry microfiber cloth. Keep away from direct moisture or high-humidity areas like bathrooms.

What is your shipping and delivery timeline?

For in-stock items, we dispatch within 48 hours. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days across India. Custom orders require 10-14 days for creation before shipping. You will receive a tracking link via email/WhatsApp once dispatched.

Do you accept returns if the item is damaged?

Yes. We take extreme care in packaging (3-layer bubble wrap + wooden crate). However, if you receive a damaged piece, please send us an unboxing video within 24 hours of delivery. We will offer a free replacement or a full refund.

Can I customize the color or size?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom orders. You can choose specific dimensions, frame colors, or mirror patterns. Visit our Custom Orders page or WhatsApp us to discuss your requirements.

Trade & Wholesale (B2B)

Do you offer trade discounts for Architects and Interior Designers?

Yes, we have a dedicated Trade Program offering exclusive pricing tiers (15-25% off) for industry professionals. Please register on our B2B Portal with your business details to access trade pricing.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for bulk pricing?

Our bulk pricing tiers start at an MOQ of 5 units. For corporate gifting or large hotel projects (50+ units), we offer deeper discounts and custom branding options.

Can I request a sample before placing a large order?

Yes, we can ship a 6"x6" sample tile or a full-size prototype. Samples are charged at retail price initially, but this cost is fully adjusted/refunded against your final bulk order.

What are the payment terms for commercial orders?

For commercial orders, we require a 50% advance deposit to begin production, with the remaining 50% due prior to dispatch. We provide GST invoices for all B2B transactions.
