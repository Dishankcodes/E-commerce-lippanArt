Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handcrafted Lippan artworks.

General & Orders

What materials are used in your Lippan Art?

Our artworks are crafted using authentic Kutch clay on durable MDF bases, finished with traditional glass mirrors (Aabhla).

How do I clean and maintain the artwork?

Use a dry microfiber cloth or soft brush. Avoid water or moisture to preserve the clay relief.

Are your artworks handmade or machine-made?

Every Auraloom artwork is 100% handcrafted by skilled artisans. No molds or machines are used, making each piece truly unique.

Will the final product look exactly like the photos?

Since each piece is handmade, minor variations in texture and mirror placement may occur. These variations are part of the authenticity of Lippan art.

Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?

Currently, we accept prepaid orders only to ensure secure processing and careful production planning.

Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or canceled within 12 hours of placing them. Please contact our support team as soon as possible.

Is wall mounting hardware included?

Yes, all artworks come with pre-installed hanging hooks for easy and secure wall mounting.

Shipping & delivery timeline?

In-stock items ship within 48 hours. Delivery takes 5–7 business days across India.

Trade & B2B

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes. Bulk pricing starts from 5 units, with custom rates for large commercial projects.

Can I request a sample?

Samples are available and fully adjustable against confirmed bulk orders.

Shipping, Returns & Care

Do you ship across India?

Yes, we ship pan-India using trusted logistics partners with protective packaging.

Do you ship internationally?

International shipping is available on request. Please contact us with your location for timelines and pricing.

What if my artwork arrives damaged?

If your artwork arrives damaged, contact us within 24 hours with photos. We will arrange a replacement or resolution.

Can Lippan art be used in humid areas?

Lippan art is best suited for indoor dry spaces. Avoid high-moisture areas to preserve the clay relief.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available Monday–Saturday, 10 AM – 7 PM IST.
